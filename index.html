About Dr. Supriya Vikhe

As a consultant dermatologist, I believe in delivering ethical, evidence-based skin and hair treatments. My practice goes beyond just surface-level corrections; I adopt a holistic approach that integrates clinical dermatology with personalised nutrition guidance.

Every patient consultation is focused on clear communication, active listening, and setting realistic, achievable goals for skin health. You can expect a professional, welcoming environment where your concerns are genuinely heard.
